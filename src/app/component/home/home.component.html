Welcome to Vocable

What is Vocable?

Vocable is a web-based toolkit designed with constructed language creators (Conlangers) in mind. However, anyone with an interest in experimenting with language can give Vocable a try.

Vocable combines several different toolsets to give you complete control over the sounds and structures of your very own conlang. Vocable aims to streamline the language creation process so that planning and design are faster and easier than ever.

Learn More

Why use Vocable?

Although several word generators, and language creation apps exist, we felt that many were either incomplete and required other tools to create a full language or the interface was simply unclear.

Vocable intends to remedy that by providing a step-by-step toolkit that is highly customizble while being easy to use.
